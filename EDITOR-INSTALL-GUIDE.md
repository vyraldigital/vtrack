# Installing vTrack — Quick Guide

Hi! Installing vTrack takes about 30 seconds. Windows shows a couple of safety
prompts because the app is distributed privately by Vyral Digital (not through the
Microsoft Store). **This is expected and safe** — just follow the clicks below.

---

## Step 1 — Download the installer
Open the link you were sent and download **`vTrack-Setup-0.5.0-A.exe`**.

## Step 2 — Run it
Double-click the downloaded file.

## Step 3 — "Windows protected your PC" (blue box)
If you see a blue window that says **"Windows protected your PC"**:

1. Click the small **"More info"** link (under the message).
2. A **"Run anyway"** button appears at the bottom — click it.

> If you don't see this box, skip to Step 4 — that's fine too.

## Step 4 — "Do you want to allow this app..." (User Account Control)
A blue/grey **User Account Control** window appears asking to allow changes.

- The publisher may show as **"Unknown Publisher"** — that's normal for a private app.
- Click **"Yes"**.

## Step 5 — Finish the installer
Click **Next / Install** through the setup window. When it's done, vTrack opens and a
**vTrack** shortcut is added to your desktop.

## Step 6 — Sign in
Log in with the email and password your manager gave you. That's it — you're done.

---

### Why the warnings?
Windows shows these prompts for any app it hasn't seen widely downloaded yet. They do
**not** mean anything is wrong. vTrack only runs with your normal user account (it is
**not** an administrator app), and it installs into the standard Program Files folder.

### Trouble?
If anything looks different or the install won't complete, screenshot what you see and
send it to your manager — don't keep clicking.
